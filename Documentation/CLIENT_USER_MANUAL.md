### Creating Your Account

1. **Visit the iShelter Platform** - Navigate to the login page

## â“ FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: How do I invite someone to see my project?**
A: Contact your project manager or iShelter support. They manage team access to projects.

**Q: Can I have multiple projects?**
A: Yes! Many clients have multiple projects. You can switch between them easily from your dashboard.

---

### Login & Access

**Q: What if I can't log in?**
A: 
1. Double-check your email and password
2. Use "Forgot Password" if you forgot your password
3. Clear your browser cache and try again
4. Contact support if problems persist

**Q: Can I use iShelter on my phone?**
A: Yes! iShelter works on mobile phones. Just open it in your browser. We're optimized for both desktop and mobile.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes, iShelter uses bank-grade security to protect your data.

---

### Projects & Budget

**Q: What if my project doesn't appear?**
A: 
1. Refresh the page
2. Log out and log back in
3. Contact your project manager to verify you're added to the project
4. Reach out to support

**Q: How is the project budget calculated?**
A: Budgets include stage costs.The task costs are a breakdown of the stage costs.

**Q: Can I change the budget?**
A: Only your project manager or admin can modify budgets. Contact them for changes.

**Q: What's the difference between "Total Budget" and "Remaining Budget"?**
A: 
- **Total Budget**: Full project cost
- **Remaining Budget**: Amount allocated to incomplete work

---

### Documents & Files

**Q: How do I upload documents?**
A: If you have permission, go to Documents â†’ Select Project â†’ Upload. Your project manager can grant upload permissions.

**Q: How long are documents stored?**
A: Documents are stored indefinitely in your project. They never expire.

---

### Billing & Payments

**Q: Why am I seeing an overdue invoice?**
A: An invoice is overdue if payment hasn't been received by the due date. Pay through the payment button for flutterwave or through the bank details sent to your email. You can also Contact your project manager to discuss payment.

**Q: How do I know how to pay an invoice?**
A: Open the invoice details page. It will show payment methods and bank details.

**Q: What's the difference between "Invoice" and "Billing"?**
A: 
- **Invoices**: Individual bills for work completed


**Q: Can I download invoices?**
A: Yes! Click the download button on any invoice to get a PDF copy.

---

### Notifications

**Q: Why am I not receiving notifications?**
A: 
1. Check your notification settings in your profile
2. Verify your email address is correct
3. Check your spam/junk folder
4. Contact support

---

### Technical Issues

**Q: The page is loading slowly. What should I do?**
A: 
1. Refresh the page (F5 or Cmd+R)
2. Clear your browser cache
3. Try a different browser
4. Check your internet connection
5. Contact support if it persists

**Q: I see an error message. What does it mean?**
A: Note the error message and contact support with the exact text. This helps us troubleshoot faster.

**Q: Does iShelter work offline?**
A: No, iShelter requires an internet connection. However, you can download documents while online for offline viewing.

---

### Account Management

**Q: How do I delete my account?**
A: Contact iShelter support. Account deletion must be approved by an administrator.

**Q: Can I have multiple accounts?**
A: You should only have one account per person. If you need access to multiple projects, ask your project manager to add you.
